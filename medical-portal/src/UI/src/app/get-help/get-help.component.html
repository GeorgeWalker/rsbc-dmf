Getting help with Driver Medical Fitness

How To Obtain A DMER Case ID

Please see options below to assist the patient in obtaining the DMER Case ID:

Contact a household member or trusted person

The patient would have received a letter in the mail informing them of a DMER requirement.

This letter contains the DMER Case ID. See image below for where the DMER Case ID is located on the letter.

Visit RSBC Driver Portal

If your patient has used the RSBC Driver Portal in the past, you can recommend they login to obtain their DMER Case ID.

Website address: www.webaddresshere.ca

Call RoadSafetyBC

If time permits: PHONE / CALL BACK WAIT TIMES CAN BE LENGTHY

Patient can call RSBC and obtain their DMER Case ID over the phone:
Local 250-387-7747 or toll free 1-855-387-7747

Hours of operation: Monday to Friday 9am - 4pm

Note : The patient is required to validate themselves on the phone with RSBC, a medical professional is unable to do this on the patient's behalf.

Call ICBC

If time permits:

Patient can call ICBC and obtain their DMER Case ID over the phone
Toll free in B.C., Canada and the U.S.: 1-800-950-1498
Lower Mainland: 604-982-2250
Greater Victoria: 250-978-8300
Other countries: 250-978-8300 (We accept collect calls.)

Hours of operation: Monday - Friday: 8:00 am to 5:00 pm

Note : The patient is required to validate themselves on the phone with ICBC, a medical professional is unable to do this on the patient's behalf.
